Virtual MRANTI World Engineering Day 2022

Engineering For Impact

World Engineering Day is a global celebration of engineering and the contributions of engineers for a better sustainable world.

In conjunction with World Engineering Day, Virtual MRANTI World Engineering Day 2022 - Engineering for Impact is jointly organised by Institution of Engineers, Malaysia (IEM) and STEM4ALL Makerspace, MRANTI to be a platform to spark action and build next-gen innovators in harnessing the power of engineering to meet Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and solve global pressing issues.

Be part of in-depth discussions on various topics of impactful engineering, witness the battle of pitches from our most innovative minds, and prototype your ideas physically at our pop-up Makerspace now.
